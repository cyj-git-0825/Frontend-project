Before Fostering a Dog or Cat

Fostering a pet is a serious responsibility. Please ensure that you are fully prepared before making a decision. Consider the following:

  • Commitment: Pets require daily attention, training, and love.
  • Financial Responsibility: Food, vet visits, and emergency care can be costly.
  • Living Space: Ensure your home allows pets and is safe for them.
  • Time Dedication: Do you have enough time to properly care for a pet?
  • Family/Roommates: All household members should agree to fostering a pet.
  • Emergency Plan: Be ready for unexpected pet-related situations.
